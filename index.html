Why Houston?

Houston, a cultural explosion...

Bursting with vitality and teeming with diversity, Houston is a vibrant fusion of creativity, talent, and premier attractions that establish its status as a world-class city. Boasting a thriving economy, stunning landscapes, and an optimistic, spirited population, it's easy to see why Houston is a sought-after international destination.

Houston hosts the world’s largest livestock exhibition and rodeo event annually bringing millions of visitors to city. Held annually since 1932, it combines world-class rodeo competitions, livestock auctions, concerts featuring top artists, carnival rides, and a variety of food and shopping experiences. The event celebrates Texas' rich agricultural heritage while raising funds for education through scholarships and grants. Typically hosted at NRG Park, it serves as a cornerstone of Houston’s cultural and community life.

More to know...

Space City, Tx

Houston, known as "Space City," is home to NASA's Johnson Space Center, a hub for human space exploration since its establishment in 1961. The center played a pivotal role in the Apollo moon landings, including the iconic Apollo 11 mission, and continues to support missions aboard the International Space Station. It is also the home of Mission Control, famously associated with the phrase, “Houston, we have a problem.” The Space Center Houston visitor complex offers interactive exhibits, astronaut encounters, and a chance to explore space artifacts, making it a major attraction and a symbol of Houston's leadership in space exploration.
